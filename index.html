About Me

Entrepreneurial and visionary leader with over 16 years of experience in the ICT sector. Proven expertise in technology, leadership, and academia, supported by a strong educational background, including a BA in Mathematics-Computer Science and an MA in Computer Engineering from the University of Prishtina. With over 14 years of lecturing experience at the Faculty of Computer Science, I have also served as vice-dean, promoting academic excellence and leadership. As the founder and CEO of DAX: Digital Application eXperts, I am dedicated to empowering organizations through innovative digital solutions. Currently, I am pursuing a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Ljubljana to further enhance my expertise and drive continuous innovation in the IT industry.
